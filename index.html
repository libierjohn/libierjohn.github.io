Welcome to Sunshine Tutoring Services!

Sunshine Tutoring Services is a student-run organisation dedicated to providing struggling students at all grade levels with easy and affordable help in key subjects. We offer tutoring in several areas in math and science, as well as in essay and research paper writing.

Established 2019

Sunshine Tutoring Services was established in 2019 by Johnathan Libier, a University of Miami student with over half a decade of experience in tutoring. Johnathan wishes to provide students with an easy, personal, and affordable tutoring option in a time when tutoring services are becoming more and more impersonal. All clients are helped on an individual basis, with considerations taken for each client's unique situation.
